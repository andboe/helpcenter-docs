In {bc-account-roles}, you can configure workload permissions for *Forecast*, including future workloads.
For example, you can give one role view-only access to all workloads and allow another role to view and edit all workloads.

New to *Forecast*? Learn xref:forecast:forecast.adoc[the basics] first.

[#configure-workload-permissions]
== Configure workload permissions

. Go to {bc-account-roles}.
. Select the role whose workload permissions you want to edit.
. Under *Forecast*, select the relevant checkboxes:
* *Create workloads*
* *Full access to requirement scripts*
. Click {btn-configure-workload-permissions}.
. In the *General* section, check the permissions you want to grant for all workloads.
. Click {btn-save-changes}.

[#configure-individual-workload-permissions]
== Configure individual workload permissions

In Enterprise WFM, you can grant permissions for individual workloads.
This lets you customize view and edit permissions by workload.

. Go to {bc-account-roles}.
. Select the role whose workload permissions you want to edit.
. Under *Forecast*, select the relevant checkboxes:
* *Create workloads*
* *Full access to requirement scripts*
. Click {btn-configure-workload-permissions}.
. In the *General* section, check the permissions that apply to all workloads.
. In the *Workload* section, check the permissions for each workload individually.
Use the search field to narrow down the workload list if needed.
. Click {btn-save-changes}.
